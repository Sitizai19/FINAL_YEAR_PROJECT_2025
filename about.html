About CatsWell

About Us

Our Mission

To provide exceptional, compassionate, and professional pet care services that ensure the health, happiness, and well-being of every pet entrusted to our care. We are committed to creating a safe, loving environment where pets can thrive and pet owners can have complete peace of mind.

Our Vision

To be the leading pet care service provider in Malaysia, recognized for our innovative approach, exceptional service quality, and unwavering commitment to animal welfare. We envision a future where every pet receives the highest standard of care and every pet owner feels confident in our expertise and dedication.

About Our Policy

At CatsWell, we maintain strict policies to ensure the highest standards of service and safety for all pets and their owners.

Health & Safety

All pets must be up-to-date with vaccinations records. We maintain strict hygiene protocols to ensure your pet's safety at all times.

Booking & Cancellation

Bookings can be made up to 30 days in advance. Cancellations must be made 24 hours prior to service.

Payment Policy

Payment is required upon booking confirmation. We accept online transfers and QR payments. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Our Rules

To ensure a safe and pleasant experience for all pets and staff, please follow these important rules and guidelines.

  • Health Requirements: All pets must have current vaccination records and be in good health. Sick pets will not be accepted for services.
  • Behavioral Standards: Pets must be socialized and non-aggressive. We reserve the right to refuse service to pets that pose a safety risk.
  • Arrival Time: Please arrive 10 minutes before your scheduled appointment. Late arrivals may result in shortened service time.
  • Personal Items: Label all personal items (toys, blankets, food). We are not responsible for lost or damaged personal belongings.
